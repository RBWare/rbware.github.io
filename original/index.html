About me.

My name is Ryan Bailey. I started developing software as a hobbyist from a very early age. I have a fervent desire for all things technology, especially in mobile. My portfolio includes several applications on varying platforms, including one of the first 2000 Android applications ever published. I have been an iOS user since the original iPhone shipped, and an Android Developer since the public launch of the m3-beta SDK in 2007. I attended Google I/O four times, once in 2010, again in 2013, in 2015 and most recently in 2016. I also attend other meetups and gatherings for developers, usually relating to Android Development and Design. I was a Google Glass Explorer, and also develop applications for Android Wear, Android TV, and have recently started working with Android Auto.
